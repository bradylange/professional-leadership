------------------------------------------------------------------------------
raw_jobs.csv
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Contains jobs scraped from Indeed.com with full details of the specific job.


------------------------------------------------------------------------------
jobs_sentences.csv
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Contains jobs scraped from Indeed.com with only job descriptions in 
sentence form and no identification for what job belongs to the sentence.
Notes:
- Sentences are formed with the criteria of punctuation followed by white 
  space then an uppercase letter
- Bullet points are considered a sentence
